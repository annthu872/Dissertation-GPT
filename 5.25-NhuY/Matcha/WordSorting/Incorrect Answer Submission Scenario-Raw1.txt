Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Incorrect Answer Submission for Word Sorting Test
objective: Verify that the system correctly identifies and responds to an incorrect answer submission by displaying a red pop-up screen.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the application as a learner.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the word sorting test.
  - Step 3: The screen displays a question with letters of a vocabulary arranged randomly.
  - Step 4: Incorrectly arrange the letters by clicking on the letter buttons in the wrong order.
  - Step 5: Press enter after finishing arranging the letters.
expectedResult: A red pop-up screen should appear, indicating that the answer is wrong.
explanation: This test case is created to ensure that the system can accurately detect when a learner submits an incorrect answer during a word sorting test and provide immediate feedback through a red pop-up screen. It directly matches the scenario name by focusing on the submission of an incorrect answer.
