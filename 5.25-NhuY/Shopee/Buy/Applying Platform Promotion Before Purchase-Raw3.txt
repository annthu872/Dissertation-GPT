Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Applying Platform Promotion Before Purchase
objective: Verify that the user can apply a platform's promotion voucher before finalizing the purchase.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the shopping cart page.
  - Step 3: Choose the product(s) to buy by clicking the tick box at the head of the product's block.
  - Step 4: Click the buy button at the right bottom of the screen to proceed to the payment page.
  - Step 5: On the payment page, verify that the default delivery location, product information, shipping information, and payment method are displayed.
  - Step 6: Click on 'Choose voucher' in the platform's promotion information section.
  - Step 7: From the list of available vouchers, choose one by clicking on it.
  - Step 8: Click the order button to finalize the purchase.
expectedResult: The chosen platform's promotion voucher is applied, and the order's price is recalculated to reflect the promotion before the user finalizes the purchase.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can successfully apply a platform's promotion to their order before purchase, which is a critical functionality for enhancing user satisfaction and encouraging sales.
