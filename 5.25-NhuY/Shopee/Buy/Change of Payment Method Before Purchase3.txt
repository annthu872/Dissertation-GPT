Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Change of Payment Method Before Purchase
objective: Verify that the user can successfully change the payment method before finalizing the purchase.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the shopping cart page.
  - Step 3: Select the product(s) you wish to purchase by clicking the tick box at the head of the product's block.
  - Step 4: Click the buy button at the right bottom of the screen.
  - Step 5: On the payment page, click on the Change title in the payment method section.
  - Step 6: From the list of available payment methods, choose a different payment method by clicking on it.
expectedResult: The system should successfully change the payment method to the newly selected option.
reflect: yes
explanation: This test case directly addresses the test scenario of changing the payment method before a purchase. It follows the steps a user would take to change their payment method at the point of purchase, from logging in, selecting products, to changing the payment method. The expected result confirms whether the system supports this functionality, making it a perfect reflection of the given test scenario.
