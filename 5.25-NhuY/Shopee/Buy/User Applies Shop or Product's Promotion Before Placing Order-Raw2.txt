Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Apply Shop Promotion Before Placing Order
objective: Verify that the user can successfully apply a shop's promotion to their order before placing it.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the shopping cart page.
  - Step 3: Choose the product(s) you want to buy by clicking the tick box at the head of the product's block.
  - Step 4: Click the buy button at the right bottom of the screen to proceed to the payment page.
  - Step 5: On the payment page, click on 'Choose voucher' in the product's information section.
  - Step 6: From the list of available vouchers, choose a shop's promotion voucher by clicking on it.
expectedResult: The chosen shop's promotion is applied, and the product's price is recalculated to reflect the promotion.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can apply a shop's promotion to their order, which is a critical functionality for enhancing user satisfaction and encouraging purchases.
