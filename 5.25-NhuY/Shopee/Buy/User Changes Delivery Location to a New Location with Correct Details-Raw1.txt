Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Change Delivery Location to a New Location with Correct Details
objective: Verify that the user can change the delivery location to a new location with correct details during the checkout process.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the shopping cart page.
  - Step 3: Select a product by clicking the tick box at the head of the product's block.
  - Step 4: Click the buy button at the right bottom of the screen to proceed to the payment page.
  - Step 5: On the payment page, click on the Change title in the delivery location section.
  - Step 6: Click the Add new location button.
  - Step 7: Fill in the name field with a correct name that contains only letters and is not less than 2 letters.
  - Step 8: Fill in the phone number field with a correct format (+84) xxx xxx xxx.
  - Step 9: Choose a city from the list provided by the platform.
  - Step 10: Choose a district from the list provided by the platform.
  - Step 11: Choose a ward from the list provided by the platform.
  - Step 12: Fill in the exact location's address in the provided field with an address available on the map provided by the platform.
  - Step 13: Select if the location is their own house or their working place.
  - Step 14: Click on the Finish button to save the location.
expectedResult: The system should save the new location and update the delivery location information in the delivery location section on the payment page.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can successfully add a new delivery location with the correct details during the checkout process. It follows the alternative flow 3 of the use case specification, focusing on changing the delivery location to a new one.
