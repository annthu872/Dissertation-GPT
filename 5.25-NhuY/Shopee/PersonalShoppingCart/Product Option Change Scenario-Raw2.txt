Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Change Product Option in Shopping Cart
objective: Verify that a user can change the option of a product in their shopping cart and the system updates the product option accordingly.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the system.
  - Step 2: Click on the shopping cart icon at the top right of the screen.
  - Step 3: Navigate to a product in the shopping cart that has multiple options available.
  - Step 4: Click on the option dropdown of the product.
  - Step 5: Select a different option from the dropdown list.
expectedResult: The system should update the product option in the shopping cart to the new selection made by the user.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can change product options directly from their shopping cart and that the system accurately reflects these changes. It directly tests the functionality described in Alternative flow 7 of the use case specification.
