Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Adjust Shipping Rating Before Submitting
objective: Verify that the user can adjust the shipping rating before submitting the product rating.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the tracking order status page and select the 'Finished' tab.
  - Step 3: Click on the Rating button of the product you want to rate.
  - Step 4: In the pop-up rating form, locate the Service rating section for shipping.
  - Step 5: Adjust the shipping rating by clicking on the desired number of stars.
  - Step 6: Click the Done button to submit the rating.
expectedResult: The system should save the adjusted shipping rating and display it in the product's detail page's rating section.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can adjust the shipping rating as part of their overall product rating before final submission, directly addressing the scenario of adjusting shipping rating.
