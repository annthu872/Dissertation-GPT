Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Rating Submission with Default Values
objective: Verify that the user can submit a rating with default values for a product
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the tracking order status page and select the 'Finished' tab.
  - Step 3: Click on the Rating button of the product you want to rate.
  - Step 4: Verify the rating form popup appears with a 5-star quality rating, 5-star seller rating, and 5-star shipping rating pre-selected.
  - Step 5: Click the Done button.
expectedResult: The system saves the rating and displays it in the product's detail page's rating section with 5 stars for quality, seller, and shipping.
explanation: This test case ensures that the system correctly handles rating submissions using the default star ratings.
