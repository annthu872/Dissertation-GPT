Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Adjust Quality Rating Before Submitting
objective: Verify that the user can adjust the quality rating of a product before submitting the rating.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the tracking order status page and select the 'Finished' tab.
  - Step 3: Click on the Rating button of the product you want to rate.
  - Step 4: In the pop-up rating form, click on the star rating in the quality rating section to adjust the quality rating to a desired number of stars (1 to 5 stars).
  - Step 5: Click the Done button to submit the rating.
expectedResult: The system should save the adjusted quality rating and display it in the product's detail page's rating section.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can adjust the quality rating of a product using the star rating system before finalizing their rating submission.
