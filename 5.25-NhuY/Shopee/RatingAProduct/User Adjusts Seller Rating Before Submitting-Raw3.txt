Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Adjust Seller Rating Before Submitting
objective: Verify that the user can adjust the seller rating before submitting the rating form.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the tracking order status page and select the 'Finished' tab.
  - Step 3: Click on the Rating button of the product you want to rate.
  - Step 4: In the pop-up rating form, adjust the seller rating by clicking on a different number of stars in the Service rating section for seller's rating.
  - Step 5: Click the Done button.
expectedResult: The system should save the adjusted seller rating and display it in the product's detail page's rating section.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can adjust the seller rating as part of their product review before final submission. It directly tests the functionality described in the alternative flow 2 of the use case specification.
