Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Order Tracking by Status Filter
objective: Verify that the user can filter orders by their status (e.g., Delivering, Finished, Cancelled) and view the filtered list.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform with valid user credentials.
  - Step 2: Click on the personal name and avatar section at the top right of the platform's page.
  - Step 3: From the personal bought order page, click on one of the tabs named after an order status (e.g., Delivering, Finished, Cancelled).
expectedResult: The system should display only the orders that match the selected status.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can filter their orders based on specific statuses, making it easier to track the progress or history of their orders.
