Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Display of Product Information
objective: Ensure that all product information is displayed correctly when a user clicks on a product card from the homepage, search page, or cart page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on any product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product title and description are displayed prominently.
  - Step 4: Check that high-quality images of the product from multiple angles are showcased.
  - Step 5: Ensure that videos demonstrating the product's features and usage are included.
  - Step 6: Confirm that detailed product specifications are listed.
  - Step 7: Verify that the current price, including any discounts or special pricing, is displayed.
  - Step 8: Check real-time stock availability.
  - Step 9: Ensure available shipping methods, estimated delivery times, and shipping fees are displayed.
  - Step 10: Verify the seller's profile, including their name, ratings, and reviews, is displayed.
  - Step 11: Confirm there is an option to contact the seller directly through the platform's messaging system.
  - Step 12: Check that a link to the seller's store page on Shopee is provided.
  - Step 13: Verify that the average rating based on customer reviews is displayed.
  - Step 14: Ensure that 3 latest detailed reviews, including ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images, are shown.
  - Step 15: Confirm that a list of related product cards is displayed.
expectedResult: All product information, including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price, stock availability, delivery options, seller information, and customer reviews, is correctly displayed along with related product cards.
explanation: This test case ensures that when a user clicks on a product card, they are presented with all necessary product information accurately, fulfilling the basic flow of the Product Information Display scenario.
