Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display from Homepage
objective: Ensure that all product information is correctly displayed when accessed from the homepage
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card displayed on the homepage.
  - Step 3: Observe the product information displayed.
expectedResult: The product information page should display all details including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price information, stock availability, delivery options, seller profile, seller contact, store link, customer reviews and ratings, and related products.
explanation: This test case ensures that users accessing a product from the homepage can view all necessary product details, matching the basic flow of the Product Information Display scenario.
