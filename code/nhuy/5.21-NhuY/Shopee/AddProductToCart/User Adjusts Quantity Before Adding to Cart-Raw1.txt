Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Adjust Quantity of a Product Without Options Before Adding to Cart
objective: Verify that a user can adjust the quantity of a product without options before adding it to the shopping cart.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to a page that contains product cards.
  - Step 3: Click on a product card to go to the product's detail page.
  - Step 4: Adjust the quantity of the product by clicking the plus or minus button in the quantity section until it reaches the desired amount.
  - Step 5: Click the add to cart button.
expectedResult: The system adds the specified quantity of the product into the user's shopping cart.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can successfully adjust the quantity of a product without options before adding it to their cart, which is a direct match to the scenario name.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Adjust Quantity of a Product With Options Before Adding to Cart
objective: Verify that a user can adjust the quantity of a product with options before adding it to the shopping cart.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in to the platform.
  - Step 2: Navigate to a page that contains product cards.
  - Step 3: Click on a product card that leads to a product's detail page with multiple options available.
  - Step 4: Choose one of the product's available options.
  - Step 5: Adjust the quantity of the product by clicking the plus or minus button in the quantity section until it reaches the desired amount.
  - Step 6: Click the add to cart button.
expectedResult: The system adds the specified quantity of the selected option of the product into the user's shopping cart.
explanation: This test case verifies the functionality for users to adjust the quantity of a product that has options before adding it to their cart, aligning with the scenario's focus on adjusting quantity before purchase.
