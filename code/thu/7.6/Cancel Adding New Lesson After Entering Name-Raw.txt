Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Cancel Adding New Lesson After Entering Name
objective: Verify that the administrator can cancel the process of adding a new lesson after entering the lesson's name.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log in as an administrator.
  - Step 2: Press the tab 'Lesson Management' on the navbar.
  - Step 3: Select 'Add new Lesson' from the dropdown menu.
  - Step 4: Enter a unique name in the lesson name field.
  - Step 5: Select 'Cancel' to stop adding a new lesson.
expectedResult: The system should cancel the process of adding a new lesson and possibly redirect the administrator to the previous page or a relevant page without creating a new lesson.
explanation: This test case ensures that administrators have the option to cancel the process of adding a new lesson after they have started it, specifically after entering the lesson's name. It tests the functionality of the 'Cancel' option in the lesson creation process.
