Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify successful deletion of an author
objective: Ensure that the user can successfully delete an author and the record is removed from the database
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the author list/details page.
  - Step 2: Locate the author you wish to delete and click the 'Delete' button next to their name.
  - Step 3: On the confirmation dialog, click the 'Confirm' button to proceed with the deletion.

explanation: This test case ensures that the deletion process works as intended, removing the specified author's record from the database upon confirmation.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify cancellation of author deletion
objective: Ensure that when a user cancels the deletion of an author, the record remains unchanged in the database
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the author list/details page.
  - Step 2: Locate the author you wish to delete and click the 'Delete' button next to their name.
  - Step 3: On the confirmation dialog, click the 'Cancel' button to abort the deletion process.
expectedResult: The author's record should remain in the database, and the user should still see the author in the author list/details page.
explanation: This test case verifies that the cancellation functionality works correctly, preventing the deletion of the author's record when the user chooses to cancel.
