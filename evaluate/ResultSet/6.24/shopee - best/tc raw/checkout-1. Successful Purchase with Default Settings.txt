  - Step 9: Click on the 'Place Order' button.
  - Step 10: Wait for the system to process the payment and confirm the order.
  - Step 11: Verify that the order confirmation and details are displayed by the system.
expectedResult: The user successfully completes the purchase with default settings, and the order confirmation is displayed.
