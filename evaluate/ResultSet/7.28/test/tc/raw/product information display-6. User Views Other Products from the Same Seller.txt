Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: View Other Products from the Same Seller via Store Link
objective: Verify that users can view other products from the same seller by clicking on the Store Link in the product information page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Locate the 'Store Link' in the seller profile section.
  - Step 4: Click on the 'Store Link' to navigate to the seller's store page.
expectedResult: The system should navigate the user to the seller's store page, displaying other products from the same seller.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can easily navigate to a seller's store page to view more products from the same seller, enhancing the shopping experience by providing easy access to a wider range of products from a trusted seller.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Expand Customer Reviews and View More Reviews
objective: Verify that users can expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section to view more reviews.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Scroll to the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 4: Click on the option to expand and view more reviews.
expectedResult: The system should expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section, displaying more detailed reviews beyond the initial 3 latest reviews.
explanation: This test case verifies the functionality of expanding the customer reviews section, allowing users to access more detailed feedback from other customers, which can help in making informed purchasing decisions.

Test Case: Test Case 3
testCaseName: Scroll to View More Related Products
objective: Verify that users can scroll to view more related product cards in the product information page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Scroll to the 'Related Products' section at the bottom of the page.
  - Step 4: Scroll within the 'Related Products' section to view more product cards.
expectedResult: The system should allow users to scroll through the 'Related Products' section, displaying additional related product cards.
explanation: This test case ensures that users have the ability to explore more products related to the one they are currently viewing, potentially increasing engagement and sales opportunities for sellers.
