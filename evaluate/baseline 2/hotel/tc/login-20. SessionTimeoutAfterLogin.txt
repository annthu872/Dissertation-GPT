Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Session Timeout for Hotel Owner after Successful Login
objective: To ensure that the session for a Hotel Owner expires after a predefined time of inactivity post successful login
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the login page.
  - Step 2: Enter valid credentials for a Hotel Owner.
  - Step 3: Click the submit button.
  - Step 4: Wait for the predefined session timeout period of inactivity.
  - Step 5: Attempt to navigate to another page within the application.
expectedResult: The system should automatically log the Hotel Owner out and redirect to the login page with a session timeout message.
