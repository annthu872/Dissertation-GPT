Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify correct answer selection in Listening Pronunciation Test
objective: To ensure that the system accurately recognizes and rewards the learner for selecting the correct answer in a Listening Pronunciation Test.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Ensure the learner is logged into the application and has navigated to the review test section.
  - Step 2: Start the Listening Pronunciation Test where the screen displays the test with a speaker button.
  - Step 3: Click on the speaker button to listen to the pronunciation of the word.
  - Step 4: Guess the entire word based on the pronunciation and choose the correct answer among the options provided.
  - Step 5: Submit the answer.
expectedResult: After submitting the correct answer, a pop-up screen should appear in green, indicating the answer is correct.
