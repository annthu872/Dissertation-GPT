Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Correct Answer Functionality in Listening Pronunciation Test
objective: Ensure that when a learner selects the correct answer in a listening pronunciation test, the system correctly identifies it and displays a green pop-up screen.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the application and navigate to the review test section.
  - Step 2: Start a listening pronunciation test question.
  - Step 3: Listen to the pronunciation by clicking the speaker button.
  - Step 4: Guess the entire word and select the correct answer.
  - Step 5: Submit the answer.
expectedResult: After submitting the correct answer, a green pop-up screen should appear indicating the answer is correct.
