Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Product Information Display Verification
objective: Verify that the product information is displayed correctly when a user clicks on a product card from the homepage, search page, or cart page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product title and description are displayed prominently.
  - Step 4: Check that high-quality images and videos of the product are showcased.
  - Step 5: Ensure detailed product specifications are listed.
  - Step 6: Confirm the display of the current price and any discounts or special pricing.
  - Step 7: Check for real-time stock availability.
  - Step 8: Verify the display of available shipping methods, estimated delivery times, and shipping fees.
  - Step 9: Ensure the seller's profile, including their name, ratings, and reviews, is displayed.
  - Step 10: Check for the option to contact the seller directly through the platform's messaging system.
  - Step 11: Verify the presence of a link to the seller's store page on Shopee.
  - Step 12: Confirm the display of the average rating, 3 latest detailed reviews with ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images.
  - Step 13: Verify that a list of related product cards is displayed.
expectedResult: All the product information including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price information, stock availability, delivery options, seller profile, seller contact, store link, customer reviews and ratings, and related products are correctly displayed.
