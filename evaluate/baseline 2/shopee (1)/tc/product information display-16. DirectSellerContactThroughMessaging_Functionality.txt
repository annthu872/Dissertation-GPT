Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display
objective: Ensure that all product information is correctly displayed including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price, stock, delivery options, seller profile, seller contact, store link, customer reviews, and related products.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Observe the product information displayed.
expectedResult: The product information should include a prominently displayed title, detailed description, high-quality images, videos demonstrating the product, detailed specifications, current price with any discounts, real-time stock availability, available shipping methods with estimated delivery times and fees, seller's profile with name, ratings, and reviews, an option to contact the seller, a link to the seller's store, the average rating from customer reviews, 3 latest detailed reviews with ratings, written feedback, user-uploaded images, and a list of related product cards.
