Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display on Product Page
objective: Ensure that all the product information is correctly displayed on the product page including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price, stock availability, delivery options, seller profile, and contact information.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product title is displayed prominently.
  - Step 4: Check that a detailed product description is provided.
  - Step 5: Ensure that high-quality images of the product are showcased.
  - Step 6: Confirm that videos demonstrating the product's features are included.
  - Step 7: Verify that detailed product specifications are listed.
  - Step 8: Check the display of the current price and any discounts or special pricing.
  - Step 9: Confirm real-time stock availability is shown.
  - Step 10: Verify available shipping methods and estimated delivery times are displayed.
  - Step 11: Check that the seller's profile, including their name, ratings, and reviews, is displayed.
  - Step 12: Ensure there is an option to contact the seller directly.
  - Step 13: Verify that a link to the seller's store page is available.
  - Step 14: Confirm the display of the average rating and 3 latest detailed reviews.
  - Step 15: Check that a list of related product cards is displayed.
expectedResult: All product information including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price, stock availability, delivery options, seller profile, and contact information are correctly displayed on the product page.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify Functionality of Expanding Customer Reviews and Questions
objective: Ensure that users can expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' and 'Customer Questions' sections to view more content.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Follow steps 1 and 2 from Test Case 1 to navigate to a product page.
  - Step 2: Scroll down to the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 3: Click on the option to expand and view more reviews.
  - Step 4: Verify that more reviews are displayed.
  - Step 5: Scroll down to the 'Customer Questions' section.
  - Step 6: Click on the option to expand and view more questions and answers.
  - Step 7: Verify that more questions and seller answers are displayed.
expectedResult: Users can successfully expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' and 'Customer Questions' sections to view additional content.

Test Case: Test Case 3
testCaseName: Verify Navigation to Seller's Store Page via Store Link
objective: Ensure that users can navigate to the seller's store page by clicking on the Store Link on the product page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Follow steps 1 and 2 from Test Case 1 to navigate to a product page.
  - Step 2: Locate the 'Store Link' on the product page.
  - Step 3: Click on the 'Store Link'.
  - Step 4: Verify that the user is redirected to the seller's store page.
  - Step 5: Confirm that other products from the same seller are displayed on the store page.
expectedResult: Users are redirected to the seller's store page where other products from the same seller are displayed.
