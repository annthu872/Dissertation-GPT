Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Display of Delivery Options on Product Information Page
objective: Ensure that the delivery options, including shipping methods and estimated delivery times, are correctly displayed on the product information page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Scroll to the 'Delivery Options' section on the product information page.
expectedResult: The 'Delivery Options' section should display all available shipping methods and estimated delivery times clearly.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify Display of Product Specifications and Price Information
objective: To check if the product's specifications and price information are accurately displayed.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: From the homepage, search page, or cart page, select a product to view.
  - Step 2: On the product information page, locate the 'Specifications' section to view product details such as dimensions, weight, material, and color options.
  - Step 3: Scroll to the 'Price Information' section to view the current price, discounts, offers, or special pricing available.
expectedResult: The 'Specifications' section should list all relevant product details, and the 'Price Information' section should accurately display the current price along with any discounts or special offers.

Test Case: Test Case 3
testCaseName: Verify Functionality of Seller Contact and Store Link
objective: To ensure users can contact the seller directly and access the seller's store page from the product information page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Access a product's information page by clicking on a product card from the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Scroll to the 'Seller Profile' section and click on the 'Seller Contact' option to initiate direct contact through the platform's messaging system.
  - Step 3: Click on the 'Store Link' to navigate to the seller's store page on the platform.
expectedResult: Clicking on 'Seller Contact' should open the messaging system to contact the seller directly. Clicking on 'Store Link' should redirect the user to the seller's store page on the platform.

Test Case: Test Case 4
testCaseName: Verify Customer Reviews and Ratings Display
objective: To confirm that the product information page displays customer reviews and ratings accurately, including the option to view more detailed reviews.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Select a product by clicking on its card from either the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: On the product information page, scroll to the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 3: Click on the option to expand and view more detailed reviews.
expectedResult: The 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section should display the average rating, show 3 latest detailed reviews, and allow users to expand the section to view more reviews.
