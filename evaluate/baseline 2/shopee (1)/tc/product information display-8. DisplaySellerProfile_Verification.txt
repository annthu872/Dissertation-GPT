Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display Including Seller Profile
objective: Ensure that all product information, including the seller's profile, is accurately displayed to the user upon clicking a product card.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on any product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product's title and description are prominently displayed.
  - Step 4: Check for high-quality images and videos showcasing the product.
  - Step 5: Confirm that detailed product specifications are listed.
  - Step 6: Ensure the current price, discounts, and special pricing are visible.
  - Step 7: Check for real-time stock availability.
  - Step 8: Verify the display of shipping methods, estimated delivery times, and shipping fees.
  - Step 9: Confirm that the seller's profile, including name, ratings, and reviews, is displayed.
  - Step 10: Ensure there is an option to contact the seller directly.
  - Step 11: Verify the presence of a link to the seller's store page.
  - Step 12: Check for the display of the overall rating, 3 latest detailed reviews with ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images.
  - Step 13: Confirm that a list of related product cards is displayed.
expectedResult: All product information, including the seller's profile, should be accurately displayed as per the specifications.
