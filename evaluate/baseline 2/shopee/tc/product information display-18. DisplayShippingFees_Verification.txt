Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display Including Shipping Fees
objective: Ensure that the product information display includes all necessary details such as title, description, images, videos, specifications, price, stock availability, delivery options with shipping fees, seller profile, and contact information.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product title is displayed prominently.
  - Step 4: Check for a detailed product description, including key features, usage instructions, and benefits.
  - Step 5: Ensure high-quality images of the product from multiple angles are showcased.
  - Step 6: Confirm the inclusion of videos demonstrating the product's features and usage.
  - Step 7: Look for a list of detailed product specifications.
  - Step 8: Check the display of the current price and any available discounts or special pricing.
  - Step 9: Verify real-time stock availability.
  - Step 10: Confirm the display of available shipping methods, estimated delivery times, and shipping fees.
  - Step 11: Check for the display of the seller's profile, including their name, ratings, and reviews.
  - Step 12: Verify the option to contact the seller directly through the platform's messaging system.
  - Step 13: Ensure there is a link to the seller's store page on the platform.
expectedResult: The product information page should accurately display all the specified details, including shipping fees, ensuring the user has all necessary information for making an informed purchase decision.
