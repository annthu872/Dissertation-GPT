Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display with Discounts Highlighted
objective: Ensure that the product information display includes all specified details with discounts and offers being prominently highlighted.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Observe the product information displayed.
expectedResult: The product information should include title, description, high-quality images, videos, specifications, current price with discounts/offers highlighted, stock availability, delivery options including shipping fees, seller profile with ratings and reviews, seller contact option, store link, customer reviews and ratings with the option to view more, and a list of related products.
