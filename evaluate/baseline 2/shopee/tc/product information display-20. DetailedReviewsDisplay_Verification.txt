Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Detailed Reviews Display on Product Information Page
objective: Ensure that the product information page displays the 3 latest detailed reviews including ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Scroll down to the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
expectedResult: The system should display the 3 latest detailed reviews with ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images under the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify Expansion of Customer Reviews and Ratings
objective: To verify that users can expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section to view more reviews.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Follow steps 1 and 2 of Test Case 1 to navigate to a product's information page.
  - Step 2: Scroll to the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 3: Click on the option to expand and view more reviews.
expectedResult: The system should expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section, allowing the user to view more detailed reviews beyond the initial 3 displayed.
