  - Step 2: Click on a product card.
  - Step 3: Verify that the product title is displayed prominently.
  - Step 4: Check that a detailed product description is provided.
  - Step 5: Ensure that high-quality images of the product from multiple angles are showcased.
  - Step 6: Confirm that videos demonstrating the product's features and usage are included.
  - Step 7: Verify that detailed product specifications are listed.
  - Step 8: Check that the current price of the product is displayed along with any discounts or special pricing.
  - Step 9: Ensure that real-time stock availability for the product is shown.
  - Step 10: Verify that available shipping methods and estimated delivery times are displayed.
  - Step 11: Confirm that the seller's profile, including their name, ratings, and reviews, is displayed.
  - Step 12: Check that an option to contact the seller directly through the platform's messaging system is provided.
  - Step 13: Ensure that a link to the seller's store page on the platform is available.
  - Step 14: Verify that the average rating based on customer reviews is displayed.
  - Step 15: Confirm that 3 latest detailed reviews are shown with ratings, written feedback, and user-uploaded images.
  - Step 16: Check that a list of related product cards is displayed.
