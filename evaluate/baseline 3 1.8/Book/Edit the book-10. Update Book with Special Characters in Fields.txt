Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Update Book with Special Characters in Title
objective: Verify that the system allows updating a book's title with special characters and displays the updated record correctly.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the book list/details page.
  - Step 2: Click the 'Update' button next to the book you wish to update.
  - Step 3: In the form displayed, enter a title with special characters (e.g., @#$%^&*()) in the 'Title' field.
  - Step 4: Fill in the remaining fields (Author, Genre, Description, Publication Date) with valid data.
  - Step 5: Press the 'Submit' button.
expectedResult: The book record is updated in the database with the special characters in the title, and the list of books displays the updated record correctly.
explanation: This test case ensures that the system can handle special characters in the book title field during an update operation, which is a part of the main scenario of editing a book.
