Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Exclude a single vocabulary from review test
objective: Verify that a learner can exclude a single vocabulary from appearing in the review test by unticking the checkbox
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the application with a learner account.
  - Step 2: Select the 'Handbook' tab on the navigation bar.
  - Step 3: Scroll through the list of vocabulary to find a specific word.
  - Step 4: Untick the checkbox next to the vocabulary word you wish to exclude from the review test.
expectedResult: The system should mark the vocabulary as excluded, indicating it will not appear in the daily review test.
explanation: This test case ensures that learners can customize their review tests by excluding specific vocabulary words. It directly tests the functionality described in Alternative flow 3 of the use case.
