Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Checkout with Changed Shipping Option
objective: Verify that the user can change the shipping option during checkout and complete the purchase successfully.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the Shopee account.
  - Step 2: Add items to the shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
  - Step 3: On the order summary page, observe the default shipping option set as Standard Express.
  - Step 4: Click on the shipping option change button to view available shipping options.
  - Step 5: Select a different shipping option from the list of available options.
  - Step 6: Review the updated total cost reflecting the change in shipping option.
  - Step 7: Click on the "Place Order" button to complete the purchase.
expectedResult: The system should update the order's total cost based on the new shipping option, process the payment, and display an order confirmation with the details including the updated shipping option.
explanation: This test case ensures that users can change the shipping option during the checkout process, which directly relates to the test scenario of checking out with a changed shipping option. It verifies the system's ability to update the order details and total cost according to the user's selection and complete the order placement successfully.
