Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Display of Product Information with High Volume of Customer Reviews
objective: Ensure that the product information page displays all required information including a high volume of customer reviews when accessed from the homepage, search page, or cart page.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card to view its details.
  - Step 3: Observe the displayed product information, focusing on the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 4: Expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section to view more reviews if available.
expectedResult: The product information page should display all the required information including title, description, images, videos, specifications, price information, stock availability, delivery options, seller profile, seller contact, store link, and especially the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section with the overall rating and at least 3 latest detailed reviews. Additionally, the option to expand this section to view more reviews should work correctly.
explanation: This test case is designed to verify that the product information page correctly displays a comprehensive set of information about the product, with a particular focus on the high volume of customer reviews, as specified in the test scenario. It ensures that users can access detailed product information and reviews, which is crucial for making informed purchasing decisions.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify Functionality of Expanding Customer Reviews Section
objective: To check if users can successfully expand the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section to view more reviews.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Follow steps 1 and 2 from Test Case 1 to navigate to a product's information page.
  - Step 2: Locate the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section.
  - Step 3: Click on the option to expand this section to view more reviews.
expectedResult: Upon clicking the option to expand, the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section should display more than the initial 3 detailed reviews, allowing the user to view additional reviews.
explanation: This test case focuses on the functionality of expanding the 'Customer Reviews and Ratings' section, which is an essential feature for users who wish to read more reviews beyond the initially displayed ones. It is directly related to the test scenario's emphasis on displaying product information with a high volume of customer reviews.
