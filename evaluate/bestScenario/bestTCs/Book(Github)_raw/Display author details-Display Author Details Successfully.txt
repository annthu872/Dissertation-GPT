Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Display Author Details Successfully
objective: Verify that the application successfully displays the details of a user-selected author from the author list page
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the author list page on the application.
  - Step 2: Identify and click on the 'Title' of a specific author from the list.
  - Step 3: Observe the information displayed on the author details page.

explanation: This test case ensures that when a user selects an author by clicking on their title from the author list page, the application correctly navigates to and displays the author's details page with specific information (id, Name, Bio) as expected. It directly matches the scenario name by testing the display functionality of author details.
