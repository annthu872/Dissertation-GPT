Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Correct Answer for Listening Pronunciation Test
objective: Verify that the system displays a green pop-up screen when the learner selects the correct answer in a listening pronunciation test.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the application with a learner account.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the review test function.
  - Step 3: Start a listening pronunciation test question.
  - Step 4: Click on the speaker button to listen to the word's pronunciation.
  - Step 5: Guess the entire word and select the correct answer based on the pronunciation.

explanation: This test case ensures that when a learner selects the correct answer in a listening pronunciation test, the system accurately recognizes the answer and provides positive feedback through a green pop-up screen.
