Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Submitting an incorrect answer in the word sorting test
objective: Verify that the system correctly identifies and responds to an incorrect answer submission by displaying a red pop-up screen.
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Log into the application as a learner.
  - Step 2: Navigate to the word sorting test.
  - Step 3: The screen displays a question with letters of a vocabulary arranged randomly.
  - Step 4: Arrange the letters in an incorrect order and press enter.
expectedResult: A red pop-up screen should appear indicating that the answer is wrong.
explanation: This test case is designed to validate the system's ability to handle incorrect answers in the word sorting test by providing immediate visual feedback to the learner. It directly addresses the 'Incorrect Answer Submission Scenario' by focusing on the alternative flow where a learner chooses a wrong answer.
