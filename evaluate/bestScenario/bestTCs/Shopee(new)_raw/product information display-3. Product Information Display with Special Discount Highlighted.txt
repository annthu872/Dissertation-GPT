Test Case: Test Case 1
testCaseName: Verify Product Information Display with Special Discount Highlight
objective: Ensure that the product information page correctly displays all relevant information including a highlighted special discount
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Navigate to the homepage, search page, or cart page.
  - Step 2: Click on a product card that has a special discount offer.
  - Step 3: Observe the product information displayed on the product's information page.
expectedResult: The product information page should display the product title, detailed description, high-quality images and videos, detailed specifications, current price with the special discount highlighted, real-time stock availability, shipping methods with estimated delivery times and fees, seller's profile with ratings and reviews, an option to contact the seller, a link to the seller's store on Shopee, and the average rating with the 3 latest detailed customer reviews. The special discount should be prominently highlighted.
explanation: This test case is created to ensure that when a user clicks on a product card, especially one with a special discount, all the necessary product information is displayed correctly with the discount being prominently highlighted. It directly matches the scenario of displaying product information with special discounts highlighted.

Test Case: Test Case 2
testCaseName: Verify Navigation to Seller's Store via Store Link
objective: To confirm that users can navigate to the seller's store page on Shopee through the store link provided on the product information page
testSteps:
  - Step 1: Follow steps 1 and 2 of Test Case 1 to navigate to a product's information page.
  - Step 2: Click on the 'Store Link' provided on the product information page.
expectedResult: The user should be redirected to the seller's store page on Shopee, where they can view other products from the same seller.
explanation: This test case verifies the functionality and accessibility of the 'Store Link' on the product information page, ensuring users can easily navigate to the seller's store on Shopee. It is related to the scenario by testing a specific feature mentioned in the use case.

Test Case: Test Case 3
